Preserving centuries-old art traditions through sustainable artisan partnerships

Preserving Heritage, Empowering Artisans

Mithila Crafts works directly with over 50 artisan families across the Madhubani and Darbhanga districts of Bihar. Our mission is to bring authentic Mithila art to homes worldwide while ensuring fair wages and sustainable livelihoods for the communities that keep these traditions alive.

Every product is handcrafted using traditional techniques passed down through generations, using natural dyes and locally sourced materials wherever possible.
